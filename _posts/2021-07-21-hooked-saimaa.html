Hooked Saimaa Brand Design

Hooked Saimaa is a company that provides fishing trips for all kinds of people. The brand is very inclusive and wishes to provide an outlet for people who want to experience Saimaa in all of its glory. I was very happy I got the chance to be a part of making the Hooked Saimaa brand. We designed the brand to have feeling of awe and mystery for nature and lake of saimaa. Especially the crown tells our respect and view of the lake.
